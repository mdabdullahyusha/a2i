IT Support Specialist (EMEA)

Job Type: Full Time

Deadline: January 20, 2022

Role Overview Application

Squarespace is looking for a Senior Software Engineer - iOS to join our small, collaborative team responsible for the end-to-end development of our iOS applications and the long-term vision of Squarespace on mobile devices. You will report to our Engineering Manager in New York City.

All Squarespace employees are working remotely at this time. We are closely monitoring the status of COVID-19 as we continue our work to build a return-to-work plan that ensures a safe return to our offices and provides flexibility for our employees.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Prototype new app and feature ideas and explore new leading mobile technologies
  • Design and implement new features, create UI, debugging, data modeling
  • Collaborate across departments with team members representing product, design, web development, and server-side engineering
  • Maintain apps, listen to community and fix issues that arise
  • Mentor software engineers at varying levels of experience

QUALIFICATIONS

  • 5+ years of experience within iOS mobile development environments
  • Proven experience with Git, Swift, Cocoa and API design
  • Knowledge of application architecture, memory management, network programming, and concurrency
  • Have published one or more applications in the Apple app store
  • Well-versed in client/server architectures
  • Passionate about end-to-end-user experience design
  • Experience mentoring other engineers, and the desire to lead

Benefits & Perks

  • Health insurance with 100% premium covered for you and your dependent children
  • Flexible vacation & paid time off
  • Up to 20 weeks of paid family leave
  • Equity plan for all employees
  • Retirement benefits with employer match
  • Fertility and adoption benefits
  • Free lunch and snacks at all offices
  • Education reimbursement
  • Dog-friendly workplace in New York office
  • Commuter benefit in the form of reduced tax (Ireland) and pretax (US)

Today, more than a million people around the globe use Squarespace to share different perspectives and experiences with the world.
